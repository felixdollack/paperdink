paperd.ink is a beautiful e-paper display board to show calendar, weather, to-do lists, notifications, custom images and much more. Its an open source, low power e-paper display device. The simple, elegant display allows you to get rid of all distractions and work peacefully. Since its open source and fully hackable, you can play around and customize for yourself!

Features

Stay connected wirelessly

FCC/CE certified WiFi & Bluetooth enable seamless connectivity.

Low power

With our low power design, you can go months without charging. Consumes <20uA in deep sleep.

Open Source & Hackable

Onboard CP2104 USB-UART converter so you can extend or customize.

Stay alert

Piezo buzzer for notifications.

Interact

4 tactile buttons for input.

Store

MicroSD card slot for storing images, data, and much more.
